Frequently Asked Questions

Are dachshunds good family dogs?

Yes, dachshunds can be great family dogs. They are loyal and affectionate, but they do best with training and patience.

Do dachshunds bark a lot?

Dachshunds tend to bark more than some breeds because they are alert and protective.

Do dachshunds need daily exercise?

Yes, they need daily walks and playtime, but it should be safe and not too intense.
